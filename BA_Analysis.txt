INITIAL LAYDOWN OF IDEAS
Business Context:
- I task the team to make an interactive kiosk in a mall that will hold a Chinese New Year themed activity where you enter the Ang Pao you received and expenses you've made with the Ang Pao that will compute you're Lucky or not. Lucky customers will receive offers and promos in the mall.

User Algorithm:
Ang Pao Section
1. Ask how many Ang Paos received
2. Generate Input Boxes for number of Ang Paos received
3. Enter values of each Ang Pao
4. Calculate total Ang Pao Received
5. Collapse Section and next Section Appears

Ang Pao Expenses Section:
1. Enter Food Expenses
2. Enter Transportation Expenses
3. Calculate total expenses

Summary Section:
1. Show summary of inputs 
2. Offer to edit past sections
3. Confirm

Lucky Section:
1. Show Lucky Status
2. Give Lucky Promo if Lucky
3. Auto reset the page after a given amount of time after the display of the Lucky status
---
Application Algorithm:
Number of Angpao letters (Increment/Decrement Operators)
- Increment to add a letter
- Decrement to remove a letter

Received Money per Ang Pao (Arithmetic Operators(Add))
- Add received money per Ang Pao 

Expenses on CNY (Assignment Operator(Sub))
- Subtract expenses from food expenses and transportation
